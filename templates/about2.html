Aim

Our aim was to provide an attractive and easy-to-use interface which can be used to acquire information related to charecteristics of various countries. Charecteristics like population-distribution, climate, demographics, types of vegetation etc. ...any information related to that country can be queried on the search bar which will give desired search results.

Motivation

In the web of data, searching produces different sites from which we have to choose to gather the information we want. Many times, a single source or site is not enough to gather all the relevant and required information. Also, data will probably be available in vastly different formats on different sites and sources. Hence, we have used semantic web which uses a Standard Representation of Data for all sources and data from all sources is joined.

Example - Number of countries having area greater than 1000000 sq.km. and having more than 3 official languages.

Scope

Anyone looking for an expansive body of international data all at one place.

This includes a wide variety of people including officials, researchers, organisations, corporations, students and teachers etc...

Important Features

  • Integrated universal data.
  • Query, represent and manage data on basis of different attributes.
  • Uses wikidata.
  • Easily exploitable data.
  • Provide links to other important databases for more detail.
